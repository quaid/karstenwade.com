In the garden of code and community,
where contributions bloom like spring flowers,
we gather not as strangers,
but as architects of something greater.

Each pull request, a conversation,
each merge, a moment of trust,
building bridges with keystrokes,
weaving connections across continents.

The source is open,
like doors flung wide in welcome,
inviting all who wish to shape,
to question, to improve, to belong.

We are not islands of expertise,
but archipelagos of shared knowledge,
where every voice matters,
every idea finds fertile ground.
